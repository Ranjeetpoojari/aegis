About Aegis

At Aegis, we believe that data science fuels the innovation that leads to disruptive technologies.
Our post graduate programs (PGPs), delivered in collaboration with IBM are designed to launch our participants’ careers on an accelerated growth path. We feature industry collaboration from amongst the foremost corporates in the domains of artificial intelligence (AI) and data sciences. Our faculty are drawn from the industry with impeccable academic background backed by a curriculum that is on a par with international standards. We follow a superior pedagogy enabled by world class infrastructure like smart boards and smart-classrooms. We firmly believe that learning is a process that is fostered both within and outside the classroom – and our participants are regularly exposed to Industrial Leadership talks as well as international events such as the Data Science Congress – events that are integrated into our training calendar. The Career Management Cell (CMC) facilitates the launching of our participants future careers. We are sanguine that our participants are not just our students…they are our future ambassadors !
